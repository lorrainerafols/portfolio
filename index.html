Answered before you ask

  • We start with a discovery call to scope your project and confirm fit. Once agreed, I send a detailed proposal with deliverables, timeline, and payment schedule. Work begins after the deposit is received. You'll receive progress updates at key milestones, and all feedback is handled in clearly defined revision rounds — no open-ended back-and-forth that drags a project past its timeline.
  • Shopify (including custom Liquid and theme architecture), WordPress (Elementor, Bricks, custom blocks), and Webflow. If your project lives on one of these platforms, we're already aligned. I also handle HTML/CSS/JS work for custom components or standalone landing pages.
  • Every project includes two structured revision rounds after the initial delivery. A revision round covers consolidated feedback on work already scoped — it's not a round to introduce new requirements. Changes outside the agreed scope are quoted and handled as a separate item, which keeps both our timelines intact.
  • Yes — my Maintenance retainer covers ongoing updates, security monitoring, performance checks, and priority turnaround. It's best suited to clients who want a single point of contact for their site rather than raising tickets with a generic support service. I keep retainer slots limited so the work stays high-quality.
  • Projects are split into two payments: 50% upfront to begin work, 50% on delivery. I invoice through Wise, which supports most major currencies with low transfer fees — no USD conversion surprises. Retainers are billed monthly at the start of each cycle.

Video calls are available for discovery and handoff, and for time-sensitive matters I'm reachable within a few hours during my working day.
